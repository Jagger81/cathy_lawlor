About Me

So, if you’re ready to gain clarity, chase your goals or overcome some of the challenges in your life, get in touch today!

Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't find the answer you're looking for in these FAQs, please do not hesitate to contact me.

1. What is coaching?

Coaching is a professional relationship designed to assist a client to identify and work towards achieving their personal or business goals or aspirations. It involves partnering with a client in a thought-provoking process that inspires them to maximise their personal and professional potential. [ILI & ICF definitions]

2. Is there a minimum amount of sessions I must sign up for?

No. I believe that sometimes people need multiple sessions and sometimes they get what they need for now in one session, so I don’t enforce a minimum number of sessions.

3. Do you only work with specific people?

No. I don’t restrict my coaching to just corporate employees/just women/moms etc.. I believe that many people have goals/challenges/struggles that can benefit from coaching. However, if you have deep issues from your past that you need to discuss, I would recommend a therapist instead.

4. What is the difference between coaching and psychotherapy/counselling?

Coaching is present and future focused, whereas counselling focuses on helping people with problems which have their origin in the past and cause problems living in the present.

5. Is there anything I need to do before our session?

We will have a connection call to ensure you understand what coaching is and is not, discuss a high level of your needs and our logistics and I will send you a short contract to read and sign beforehand. Typically you will know what you want to bring to a session, but if you are not sure, we will get clarity on this at the start of the session.

6. Will you give me specific advice on how to achieve my goals?

We are all individual and we have different challenges, values, beliefs, thoughts, frames of reference, motivations etc. Coaching is all about putting you fully in the driving seat of your own life. You will often know (even if it’s deep down) what you need to do and what will work in your specific life. I will help you understand if something is holding you back and find the motivation to go after what you want. If relevant, I may use exercises, tools or refer to resources I’m aware of that may add value for you.

7. Will my sessions be confidential?

Absolutely. Your sessions are held in a completely confidential and non-judgemental space in line with the International Coaching Federation Code of Ethics, of which I am a member.
